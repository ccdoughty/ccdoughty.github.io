Caitlin C. Doughty

Hi! I'm an astronomy postdoc at Leiden Observatory in the Netherlands, and I study the epoch of hydrogen reionization using cosmological simulations. For a quick summary of my work, please see my publication list.

Current research

Currently I am using cosmological hydrodynamic simulations to study the IGM during reionization, using the Nyx and MP-Gadget codes.
